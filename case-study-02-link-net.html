Overview

The Challenge

Link Net — one of Indonesia's leading fiber internet providers — needed a cost-efficient Google Ads SEM strategy to generate qualified leads for their residential fiber packages across target service areas.

The challenge: ISP SEM is fiercely competitive. Keywords like "internet fiber" and "WiFi rumah" are crowded with large telco brands bidding aggressively, making cost-per-lead efficiency critical for a meaningful ROI.

The goal was to deliver high-intent form submissions — not vanity clicks — within a defined IDR 35M budget across three months, with a clear CPL target.

→ Bid strategy management (manual CPC → Target CPA transition)
→ Landing page feedback to optimize for Quality Score
→ Monthly reporting with CPL tracking and optimization recommendations
→ Geographic targeting refinement based on Link Net service coverage

Approach

SEM Strategy

01
Intent-Layered Keyword Architecture
Built a tiered keyword structure separating high-intent commercial terms (e.g. "pasang internet fiber") from informational queries. Applied exact match for hottest terms and phrase match for volume, with aggressive negative keyword management to protect budget from irrelevant queries.
02
Ad Copy Testing Framework
Ran 3 RSA (Responsive Search Ad) variants per ad group with distinct value props: speed-focused ("50 Mbps Tanpa Batas"), price-focused ("Internet Rumah Mulai 200K"), and reliability-focused ("Fiber Optik, Sinyal Stabil"). Speed messaging consistently outperformed by 22% CTR.
03
Bid Strategy Progression
Started Month 1 on Manual CPC to accumulate conversion data, transitioned to Target CPA bidding in Month 2 once 30+ conversions were recorded. This Smart Bidding shift drove a 18% improvement in CPL from Month 1 to Month 3.
04
Geographic & Schedule Optimization
Mapped Link Net's fiber coverage zones and restricted targeting to serviceable areas only — eliminating wasted spend on clicks from out-of-service regions. Applied bid adjustments boosting spend during 7–9pm peak home-internet search windows.

Campaign Timeline

Month-by-Month Progress

M1
Month One — Setup & Data Collection
Manual CPC · Foundation Building
Launched with Manual CPC bidding across 5 core ad groups. Collected initial Quality Score data, identified top-performing keywords, and ran first round of ad copy A/B tests. Initial CPL landed above target — expected during data accumulation phase.
M2
Month Two — Transition & Optimization
Target CPA Activated · Negative Expansion
Transitioned to Target CPA Smart Bidding after hitting 30+ conversion threshold. Expanded negative keyword list by 40 terms, paused 3 underperforming ad groups, and introduced ad schedule bid adjustments. CPL began declining toward target.
M3
Month Three — Scale & Efficiency
IDR 34K CPL Achieved · 1,020 Total Leads
Smart Bidding matured and delivered best-in-campaign efficiency. Final month generated the highest lead volume with the lowest CPL. Total campaign: 500K+ impressions, 1,020 qualified form submissions, IDR 34K average CPL across full 3-month run.
Takeaways

Key Learnings

🎯
Patient bidding strategy is a performance multiplier
Starting with Manual CPC and only switching to Smart Bidding after collecting meaningful conversion data was the critical decision. Jumping to Target CPA too early — before Google's algorithm has sufficient signal — leads to wasted spend. The Month 1 foundation made Month 3's efficiency possible.
🗺️
Geographic precision is non-negotiable for ISPs
ISP campaigns bleed budget on leads from non-serviceable areas. Mapping Link Net's actual fiber coverage zones and restricting targeting accordingly eliminated a significant source of wasted clicks. This geographic filter alone improved lead quality noticeably.
✍️
Speed messaging dominates for fiber internet categories
Testing three messaging pillars revealed that speed-centric ad copy outperformed price and reliability messaging by 22% in CTR. Indonesian consumers searching for home fiber were primarily motivated by speed promises — a category-specific insight that informed all future copy iterations.
