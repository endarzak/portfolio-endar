Overview

The Challenge

Instaskin, a growing aesthetics clinic, needed to generate a consistent flow of qualified WhatsApp consultations from TikTok Ads — with an ultra-lean daily budget of IDR 100,000 per day.

The healthcare and aesthetics category on TikTok presents a unique challenge: content must build trust quickly, comply with platform policies around medical claims, and drive a direct action (WhatsApp chat) rather than a passive click.

Success meant proving TikTok could work for a local clinic at minimal spend — and doing it fast, with measurable results from day one.

Strategy

How I'm Built This

01
Healthcare-Native Creative Framework
Developed a creative brief structured around TikTok's native content style: problem-hook (skin concern), transformation teaser (before/after framing), soft social proof, and a low-friction CTA (WA chat — not form). This approach sidesteps TikTok's restrictive medical claim policies while still building urgency.
02
Audience Stacking for Precision Reach
Combined TikTok interest targeting (Skincare, Beauty & Personal Care, Health & Wellness) with behavioral signals (beauty content engagers, online shoppers for skincare). Added a lookalike audience layer seeded from Instaskin's existing customer WhatsApp list.
03
WhatsApp as Conversion Destination
Instead of routing users to a landing page (adding friction), set WhatsApp chat as the direct conversion event via TikTok's Click-to-WhatsApp ad format. This frictionless path — from video ad to direct consultation — was a key driver of the IDR 75/conversion efficiency.
04
Lean Budget Architecture
At IDR 100K/day, budget management matters enormously. Concentrated spend in a single tight ad group initially to help TikTok's algorithm learn faster, avoiding budget fragmentation across multiple campaigns that would slow the learning phase at low daily spend levels.

Takeaways

Key Learnings

⚡
TikTok delivers fast signal — if setup is right
Unlike Google Ads where learning phases take weeks, a properly structured TikTok campaign signals quality within 24-72 hours. A 9.71% CTR from day one isn't luck — it's the result of precise audience targeting, platform-native creative, and the right conversion event. The setup work before launch determines early performance more than any mid-campaign optimization.
💬
WhatsApp as CTA removes the biggest drop-off point
For local service businesses, directing users to a form or website introduces friction and a significant drop-off rate. WhatsApp Click-to-Chat eliminates the middle layer — the user moves directly from TikTok video to active conversation with the clinic. This single UX decision is what makes IDR 75/conversion possible at IDR 100K/day.
🧠
Lean budgets require concentrated structure, not spread
The instinct with small budgets is to spread across multiple campaigns to "test everything." The opposite is true: TikTok's algorithm learns faster with concentrated spend in one focused ad group. At IDR 100K/day, every rupiah needs to feed a single learning pool — not be diluted across fragmented campaigns that never exit the learning phase.
